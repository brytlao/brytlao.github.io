Engineering

I've been involved in various projects that need prototyping and development.
Most of these projects require logging of sensor information and interaction with users.
The relevant tools are included in the descriptions.

Myo Armband

The Myo is an armband with EMG and IMU sensors, developed by Thalmic Labs (now North). I interface it with other sensor systems, such as Kinect and Lego Mindstorms. The signal quality is sufficient for interactive applications.
Tools: Myo Connect, Processing

  • See more

KinectV2

The Kinect is a motion sensing input device developed by Microsoft. It serves as a nice subsitute for the Mocap system in terms of portability and price. I interface it with other sensor systems, such as Myo and Mac3D.
Tools: KinectV2, Processing, VisualC#

  • See more

Leap Motion

The Leap Motion is a hand and finger motion sensor, developed by Leap Motion, Inc. In a work with NTT R&D, I developed a haptic feedback system similar to the Sony DualSense controller.
Tools: Leap Motion, VisualC#

  • See more

Motion Capture

Motion Capture (Mocap) is the process of recording movement. I was in charge of a nice motion capture studio, and I have 4+ years' experience in motion capture: collecting, post-processing, development, analysis.
Tools: Mac3D, nMotion, Matlab

  • See more

Computer Vision

I use machine learning techniques for computer vision. I designed an internship for high school students, and occasionally make personal projects.
Tools: PoseNet, TensorFlow, JavaScript

  • See more

Pressure Glove

The Pressure Glove was developed mainly using the ShokacChip, a multi-axis tactile sensor. I was in charge of developing a finger-pressure logging system that communicates with other devices, such as Mac3D.
Tools: ShokacChip, Processing, CAD, Opcode

  • See more

Mobile App Dev

I lead a team to develop LowCal, the Philippines' first hospital-backed preventive health care application. The app primarily functions as a diet-tracking app using a localized food database. (In collaboration with Voyager Innovations and Cardinal Santos Medical Center) [Press].
Tools: Android, Eclipse

Shepherding Simulation

Shepherding describes the dynamic interaction between a dog and a flock of sheep. The dog needs to herd the flock to a target location while collecting sheep that stray from the flock. The original model was proposed by Strombom et al.
Tools: Matlab
